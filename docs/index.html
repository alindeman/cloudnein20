Student discount

Your talent and enthusiasm earn you a discount to CloudNein in Berlin! If you follow the next steps you can get a student discount:

Step 1. Have a valid Student Card

Step 2. Show us your attempt for using SRE tooling, and there are many options out there

Step 3. Send us an e-mail to event@gophercon.berlin with:

A confirmation you have a student card that is valid at least until the conference date and a link to the repository. After all the steps are completed, you will receive your discount code which will allow you to buy in the reduced price! In order for the entrance to be valid, you must present your student card at the conference.
